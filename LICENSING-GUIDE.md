## Common Questions

### "Can I use this for free?"

**Yes, if you:**
- Are a student or learner
- Are building for a non-profit organization
- Are contributing to open-source
- Are NOT generating revenue
- Are NOT a registered business

**No, if you:**
- Generate any revenue from the software
- Use it commercially
- Are a registered business
- Build platforms for paying clients

---

### "What if I'm just testing it?"

**Free Tier applies if:**
- Testing is local/internal only
- NOT testing with real customers
- NOT accepting real revenue
- Your company doesn't use it commercially

**Commercial License needed if:**
- Testing with real users
- Accepting real transactions
- Revenue is flowing through the platform (even in test)

---

### "I'm a startup - do I need to pay?"

**Yes.** If your startup is registered and generating any revenue (even ₹1), you need a Commercial License.

---

### "Can I use this for an internal tool at my company?"

**It depends:**
- **FREE:** Internal non-revenue tool (expense tracking, scheduling, etc.)
- **PAID:** Tool that generates revenue or is offered to external customers

---

### "What's the minimum revenue to need Commercial License?"

**ANY revenue triggers Commercial License requirement.**
- ₹1 = Commercial License required
- ₹0 = Community License applies

**There's no minimum threshold.**

---

### "Can I sell consulting/implementation services using this software?"

**No, not without Commercial License.**

Even if you're selling implementation services (not the software directly), you're generating revenue from the software, so Commercial License is required.

---

### "Can I white-label this?"

**No, not without Commercial License and disclosure.**

If you want to rebrand or white-label:
1. You need Commercial License
2. You must disclose to your customers that it's based on E-Commerce Software
3. You must maintain all attribution requirements

---

### "What if I modify all the code - do I still need a license?"

**Yes.** Even with 100% rewritten code, if you started from this Software, you need proper licensing:
- Community License (free) if non-commercial
- Commercial License (paid) if commercial

---

### "Can I use this in a SaaS platform?"

**Yes, but you need Commercial License.**

If your SaaS platform charges customers, you must:
1. Obtain Commercial License
2. Pay 5-10% monthly royalty
3. Maintain attribution
4. Report revenue monthly

---

### "What about academic research?"

**Community License (FREE) applies if:**
- It's genuinely for research purposes
- Not commercialized
- Not deployed as paid service

**Commercial License needed if:**
- Research is commercialized
- Deployed as product/service
- Revenue is generated

---

### "Can I use both licenses somehow?"

**No.** You must choose ONE:
- Community License (free, non-commercial)
- Commercial License (paid, commercial)

You can't have a hybrid or use both simultaneously.

---

### "What if my company has no revenue yet?"

**Pre-revenue startups still need Commercial License if:**
- You are registered as a business
- You intend to generate revenue
- You have investors

**Community License applies if:**
- Truly educational/learning project
- Non-profit
- No business registration

## How to Prove Your Use Case

### For Community License, keep:
- University/college enrollment records
- Project code (unpublished, non-revenue)
- Documentation showing non-commercial intent
- Non-profit registration (if applicable)

### For Commercial License, prepare:
- Business registration documents
- Revenue reports & bank statements
- Deployment documentation
- Customer agreements showing revenue

## Late Payment Impact

### Don't miss the 15th!

```
Payment Due: April 15, 2026 (₹20,000)

Scenario 1: Paid on April 15
  Amount: ₹20,000 ✅

Scenario 2: Paid on April 18 (3 days late)
  Amount: ₹20,000 + (3 × 2%) = ₹21,200
  Extra cost: ₹1,200

Scenario 3: Paid on April 25 (10 days late)
  Amount: ₹20,000 + (5 × 2%) + (5 × 5%) = ₹25,500
  Extra cost: ₹5,500

Scenario 4: Not paid by May 15 (30+ days)
  Status: License SUSPENDED
  Result: Service access revoked immediately
```

## Still Unsure?

   - YES → Commercial License
   - NO → Next question

2. **Is my organization registered/incorporated?**
   - YES → Commercial License (you generate business revenue)
   - NO → Next question

3. **Is this for learning, hobby, or non-profit?**
   - YES → Community License ✅
   - NO → Commercial License
