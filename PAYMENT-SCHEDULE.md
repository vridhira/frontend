## 💰 Monthly Royalty Calculation

### Step 1: Calculate Your Gross Revenue for the Month

**Gross Revenue Includes:**
- All customer sales and transactions
- Subscription/membership fees
- Service charges and commissions
- Platform fees collected from sellers
- Transaction fees retained

**Gross Revenue EXCLUDES:**
- Sales tax collected (pass-through)
- Refunds and chargebacks
- Business expenses
- Cost of goods sold

### Step 2: Determine Your Applicable Rate

**Check your annual revenue run rate:**

```
If monthly average × 12 = ₹0 - ₹50 Lakhs/year → 5% rate
If monthly average × 12 = ₹50 Lakhs+/year     → 10% rate
```

### Step 3: Calculate Monthly Royalty

```
Monthly Royalty = Gross Revenue × Applicable Rate %

Example:
  Monthly Revenue: ₹3,00,000
  Annual run rate: ₹36,00,000 (below ₹50 lakhs)
  Rate: 5%
  Monthly Royalty Due: ₹15,000
```

### Step 4: Account for Previous Balance

```
Total Payment Due = Monthly Royalty + Any Previous Month Arrears
```

## 📈 Tiered Rate Transition

### When Annual Revenue Crosses ₹50 Lakhs

When your annual revenue run-rate reaches ₹50 lakhs, your rate increases:

```
BEFORE:  ₹50L/year → 5% each month
AFTER:   ₹50L/year → 10% each month (going forward)

Example transition:
  Jan-Nov: ₹3L/month @ 5% = ₹15,000/month
  Dec: Revenue hits ₹36.5L for year

  Jan (next year): Revenue run rate is now ₹60L/year
  → Rate increases to 10%
  → Jan payment: ₹3L @ 10% = ₹30,000
```

**No retroactive recalculation:** You don't pay back 5% months at the higher 10% rate.
